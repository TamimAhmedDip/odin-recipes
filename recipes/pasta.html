White Sauce Desi Pasta Recipe

Who haven't heard of white sauce pasta? This is one of the first recipes I ever made with my mom and hence I'll forever remember this. This is nothing like a restaurant's. But in a good way. Let's dive in.

Its a desi style extremely homemade. The white sauce is also made from scratch. It all started when I found some pasta in the open kacha bazar of Savar. Previously shop owners did not even know what a pasta is. This sparked my interest and hence we bought the pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Chicken
  • Egg
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Add some oil in the frypan, add the egg and stir
  3. Add some precoocked chicken and stir
  4. Add the pasta to the mix
  5. Take some oil in another pan
  6. Add some flour and stir quickly
  7. Once the flour becomes brown add milk and stir
  8. Once milk thickens add salt
  9. Finally add the pasta-egg-chicken mix we prepared earlier and serve!
  10. Enjoy and be grateful to Allah swt
